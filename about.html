Built by an Insider, for Creators.

PublishMint was founded by Kousar Alom Laskar, a self-published author and digital product advertiser. I began publishing books across Amazon, IngramSpark, and Google Play Books, learning firsthand what works in digital distribution.

Later, I worked behind the scenes promoting digital products as an advertiser across marketplaces like Digistore24, JVZoo, Clickbank, Awin, ShareASale, and WarriorPlus.

Through years of experience with affiliates and product creators, I identified a gap: influencers with strong audiences struggled to create simple, profitable digital assets.

PublishMint bridges that gap.

We combine affiliate marketplace data with deep audience analysis to build digital products to resell that already have proven demand.
